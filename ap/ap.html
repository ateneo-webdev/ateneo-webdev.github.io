Webdev, short term for Web Development Team, was founded by Fr. Holscher, S.J. to create a website for Ateneo de Manila High School. He pulled out some students from Livewire, now recognized as Ateneo Computer Club, who were very good in web development, to be part of this new organization in the school back then.

Today, Webdev, has been training creative and dedicated students to develop their skills in web development. HTML, CSS, Javascipt, PHP, and many more have been taught to these students, making them globally competitive compared to others.
